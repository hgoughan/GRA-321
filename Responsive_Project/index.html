Founded in 1991 Charlie's Café, is one of Sun Devil Dining’s local
                   restaurant partners, brings the taste of Europe to ASU! Proudly serving
                   Lavazza Italian espresso and featuring fresh European sandwiches and baked
                   goods. Come experience the difference! With three locations on campus, there
                   is always a Charlie's Café near! Charlie’s Café is located at the Hayden Library,
                   Bio Design B Building and the College of Design.
